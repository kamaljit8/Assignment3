Skittles Candies

Skittles are a type of fruit-flavored candy that was first introduced in the United States in 1979. They are made by the Wrigley Company, a subsidiary of Mars, Inc. Skittles come in a variety of flavors, including strawberry, orange, lemon, green apple, and grape.

Flavors

  • Strawberry
  • Orange
  • Lemon
  • Green apple
  • Grape

History

Skittles were first produced in Britain in 1974 and were later introduced in the United States in 1979. Originally, Skittles were only available in the United Kingdom, but they eventually became available in other countries as well.
